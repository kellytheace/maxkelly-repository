Welcome - Homepage

A collection of tools and models I've built for mathematical and financial analysis. Chess engines and other simulations are also included.

This site serves as a personal library for showcasing coding projects focused on mathematical challenges, actuarial science, financial modeling & data analysis - all handwritten in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, with other programming languages used for specific projects.

In addition, this collection also features chess engines, simulations & models. Each project includes code, a short write-up, and visual outputs when relevant.

Featured work

Chess Engline
Looks a few moves ahead with minimax and ranks positions using basic piece values and mobility.
Black Hole Simulator
A C program that visualizes gravitational lensing around a black hole by numerically tracing light rays near the photon sphere and event horizon.
Pension Fund Longevity & Inflation Stress Model
Tests pension solvency under stagflation-style conditions from the 1970s or unexpected longevity increases.
Survival Probability & Mortality Forecasting
An R project that fits the Lee–Carter model to historical mortality, builds survival and life-expectancy tables, and produces forward projections with uncertainty bands—ready for annuity pricing, reserving, and longevity risk stress tests.
