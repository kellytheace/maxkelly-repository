The formula for Discounted Cash Flow is:

\[ \text{DCF} = \sum_{t=1}^{n} \frac{CF_t}{(1 + r)^t} \]

Where:
- \( CF_t \): Cash flow at time \( t \)
- \( r \): Discount rate
- \( n \): Total number of periods
