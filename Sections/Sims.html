Simulations, Predictive Models & Engines

Chess engines: The following chess bots look ahead several moves, use search with pruning to skip weak lines, and score positions (material, mobility, king safety). Each version goes deeper/faster, and I have benchmarked them head-to-head to show strength gains.

Predictive models: These projects learn patterns in historical data to forecast outcomes—for example, predicting Premier League results from recent form, xG and venue.

Economic simulators: Interactive models that let you change interest rates, QE/QT, or fiscal shocks and see the knock-on effects on inflation, GDP, unemployment, and bond yields.
